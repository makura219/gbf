Update v1.1!

A whole bunch of new settings! In Notification Settings, you can turn on/off desktop notifications, choose how big they are, turn on/off sound notifications, choose what sound is played and their volume. Expect a lot more GBF characters to appear in that list of sounds! If you want to see the message that the raid poster has added, that is availible in the "Full" info level option. In Layout Settings, you can choose the amount of info is shown in each row, when a raid listing expires, and how many raids to show at once.

Note: the button for switching between the current horizontal layout and a more vertical layout like aikats is disabled for now and will be availible in the next update.
